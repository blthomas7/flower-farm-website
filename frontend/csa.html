What is CSA?

Community Supported Agriculture (CSA) is a direct relationship between you and our farm. Subscribe to receive a box of fresh, seasonal flowers every week or bi-weekly. You get the freshest flowers, and we get reliable income to grow more beautiful blooms!

Membership Options

  • 2-3 flower varieties
